KACHUNK! VOOMAROOMA!

Don Martin words are sound effects created by Don Martin, beloved American cartoonist. Two of my favorites are "kachunk", the sound of a prince jumping onto Rapunzel's hair, and "voomarooma", the sound of a man going downhill in a two-wheel device. For this project, I used Gill Sans, Futura, and Helvetica.
