This website was created for an exercise in designing a single page light and dark mode experience. For this project, I chose to create a digital viewing room for the iconic masterpiece from the late Middle Ages: the Unicorn Tapestries. I animated and stylized the layout to captivate the user's attention while paying homage to these incredible works of art.

DARK MODE






COLOR PALETTE

My chosen color palette comes from the featured image The Unicorn in Captivity. I wanted to use colors from the source itself, so the viewer could experience the rich and beautiful tones of the woven tapestry, even when viewing from a screen.










TYPE

It is speculated these tapestries were commissioned to celebrate a marriage. Medieval poets interpreted these works as a story of love and devotion. With this in mind, I chose a romantic display typeface for my design.
