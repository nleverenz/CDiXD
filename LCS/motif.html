SVGs & TYPE

+ SEE THE LIVE SITE

Our task was to experiment with trending type treatment to lay out your navigation and create a design using a variety of vector shapes. Additionally, we had to assign at least 3 different classes to at least 3 different sets of svg objects and design different hover states for them. I created SVGs of iconic motifs from history's most well known masterpieces: Vermeer's pearl earring, Monet's lilies, Matisse's cutouts, Kusama's dots, Basquiat's crown, Botticelli's oranges, Van Gogh's stars, and Warhol's soup can.

LANDING SCREEN







NAVIGATION

I used a fix vertical navigation so users could click on the text to be directed to their corresponding motif and masterpiece.









SVGs & HOVER

I added hover states to the SVGs so the viewer could interact with the motif.
