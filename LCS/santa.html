DEAR SANTA

UI DESIGN



Dear Santa, is an app redesign for the USPS Operation Santa charitable giving program. Current donors are deterred by outdated holiday charity platforms with time consuming seasons and limited options. Dear Santa, is a single platform that supports people who would like to contribute to children in need during the holiday season.

SCREENS



My design goals are to provide quick donation options, online letter adoption, direct delivery, intuitive sorting systems, push notification reminders, automatic tax refund receipts, real time proof of impact, group/corporate letter adoptions, and media outreach initiatives.

CLICK SANTA TO EXPLORE THE PROTOTYPE







COLOR PALETTE

My chosen palette features bright, charming, and cheerful hues. The brand attributes I came up with in the brainstorming stage were about capturing the positive, hopeful, and celebratory aspects that emphasize the spirit of the holiday season.










TYPE

For my design, I prioritized legibility and simplicity. I chose two sans serif typefaces: Overpass and Alegreya Sans. For titling, I used Overpass (Bold) in 24 pt. For body text I used Alegreya Sans (Regular) in 16 pt.
